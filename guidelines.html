Guidelines for Paper Submission

  1. High-quality full papers which at the time of submission, are not under review and have not already been published or accepted for publication elsewhere are solicited.
  2. The first page should include the paper's title, the abstract, a list of keywords indicating the paper's topic area(s), the authors' full names, affiliations, and e-mail addresses.
  3. All accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings.
  4. Authors are required to submit fully formatted papers (PDF & Word), with graphs, images, and other special areas arranged as intended for the final publication. Papers should be written in English conforming to the IEEE standard conference format (8.5" x 11" - US letter, Two-Column).
  5. The final manuscript for publication will be limited to 6 IEEE pages.
  6. Your paper must be printable in order to be accepted.
  7. At least one of the authors of each accepted paper has to register at the regular rate (non-student).
  8. ICESBM 2016 Organizing Committee reserves the right not to include in any paper into the proceedings that are not presented at the conference.
